Benvenuti
Director: Paolo Caracciolo, raised in Caserta.
DOP: Erme Rivezzi, born and raised in Caserta.
Music: Domenico Cimarosa, born and raised in Caserta.
The Royal Palace of Caserta extends above the surface as it does below, it is more than two million cubic meters of extension which makes it the worldâ€™s largest royal residence, but it is also one of the least visited. The cultural value of the monument is high, the problem therefore lies in its communication design.
In search of the most appropriate communication strategy, I traveled to Parma to attend the fair on the most famous typographer ever existed: Giambattista Bodoni. There I discovered that Bodoni, as well as being contemporary with respect to the construction of the building, has also worked for the rulers, font selection was therefore clear.
The logotype, clean and simple for improved adaptation to any context, was built in reference to the typesetting era: Bodoni was able to build movable metal typefaces very close together, in this case, 0 kerning occurs between R and T.
